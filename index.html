About me

I received the master degree from the department of Automation in Tsinghua University in 2021, and the B.S. degree in Automation from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics in 2015. Before the start of my master study, I worked as an electrical engineer & control engineer in a port company for almost 3 years, where I was involved in several interesting projects cocerning programmable logic controller (PLC) and embedded system. Since the beginning of May 2022, I am now luckily to start my PhD study under the supervisior of Prof. Hans-Andrea Loeliger.

I am tring to conduct more interesting researchs in signal processing, machine learning and optimization via the help of linear Gaussian models with unknown variances (NUV).

Research

My research interests include

  • factor graphs, state space models, norm with unknown variance (NUV)

  • statistical signal processing (blind source separation, nonparametric estimation)

  • machine learning (independent component analysis, boosting)

  • the application of embedded system, FPGA, and SoC in signal processing and machine learning
